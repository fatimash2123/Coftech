What is Jahia Solution Group for?

Jahia Solutions Group aims to make digital experiences simpler. Built on a cloud-based platform, Jahia helps organizations leverage their content and customer data to fully engage with their customers. Jahia integrates into existing technology stacks and can be customized to meet each organization’s specific business needs and challenges. Founded in 2002 and headquartered in Switzerland, Jahia has offices in Boston, Toronto and Paris. The vendor states Jahia’s customer community includes hundreds of global brands and organizations, including Ben & Jerry’s, Nationwide, NASA and General Motors.
