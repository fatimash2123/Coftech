10 Tech Blogs You Need to Follow in 2021

Your goal in 2021: build a successful career in the technology industry. To achieve your goal, you need to stay up to date on tech news. As part of your efforts, you need to follow the right tech blogs. There is no shortage of tech blogs at your disposal. But, some stand out from the pack, particularly for those who are ready to grow their career in the tech space. Now, let’s look at 10 tech blogs you need to follow in 2021. 1. TechCrunch TechCrunch is one of the most prominent technology blogs. It provides ongoing updates on startup and tech news. Plus, it provides a terrific resource if you want to stay in the loop regarding artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), and other state-of-the-art technologies. TechCrunch even has the Equity podcast, which focuses on venture capital stories across the tech industry. 2. GigaOm GigaOm specializes in “technical wisdom.” It is designed to help IT decision-makers understand technical challenges and find ways to solve them. As such, GigaOm provides access to a wealth of strategic, engineering-led technology research. 3. Ars Technica Ars Technica is a can’t-miss blog for experienced and aspiring technologists and IT professionals. It delivers tech news, along with op-eds, product reviews, and other informative content. Unlike many other tech blogs, Ars Technica provides long thought-pieces and in-depth explainers, too. 4. TweakTown TweakTown was launched in 1999 and has grown leaps and bounds since its inception. The blog primarily offers tech articles and product reviews. It also provides an Ask the Expert section, which gives you an opportunity to ask questions and engage with tech industry experts. TweakTown allows you to post tech questions and comments to forums as well. 5. VentureBeat VentureBeat offers insights into transformative tech news and events. It is a leading authority on AI and ML and provides extensive coverage of the gaming industry. In addition, VentureBeat publishes tech articles from a wide range of perspectives. 6. Computerworld Computerworld helps business professionals use web, mobile, and desktop applications to build successful companies. Furthermore, it provides tips and recommendations to help IT managers optimize their client systems. 7. Ubergizmo Ubergizmo is a trusted provider of honest and unbiased consumer tech product reviews. Moreover, Ubergizmo writers often add a touch of humor to their content. So, Ubergizmo blog posts are sure to make you smile. 8. ZDNet ZDNet provides 24/7 tech news and analysis. It’s a one-stop shop for national and international tech industry updates. Also, ZDNet delivers insights into tech industry trends you won’t find elsewhere. 9. TNW TNW, aka The Next Web, operates at the heart of technology. Along with providing tech news and events, TNW offers case studies that allow you to see how it helps businesses drive innovation. 10. TechSpot TechSpot is a popular blog for tech enthusiasts of all ages. It offers tech news, product reviews, and feature articles. TechSpot even provides videos, downloads, and tech trivia as well.
