Web Portal

Web portal software is an online tool allowing companies secured, unified, and personalized access to enterprise data and applications. This creates a better and more efficient communication portal between businesses, customers, and internal employees.

Internet of Things

The Internet of Things (IoT) describes a network of physical objects that are connected by embedded sensors, software, and communications technologies to exchange data with other devices and systems using the internet.
